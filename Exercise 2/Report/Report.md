### Why are the total numbers in year 2015 and 2018 so low?

Because tracking started not until June in 2015 and ended in September 2018. In these yaers we have not data of twelve months.

- First offense tracked: 2015-06-15 00:00:00
- Last offense tracked: 2018-09-03 21:25:00

## Are there (a) times, (b) days or (c) months when more serious crimes ('Part One') occur?

![Crimes heatmap](crimesHeatmap.png)

Serious crimes happen mostly in the night hours from 1 to 8 am.

On weekdays from 1 to 6 am and on weekends from 4 to 7 am.

![Crimes each day](crimesEachDay.png)

On fridays happen the most crimes and on sunday the less.

![Crimes each month](crimesEachMonth.png)

More at night.
